ABOUT GEENATION

Geenation is a private multifaceted company aimed at enacting new distinctive blue prints in the Multimedia, Entertainment, ICT, Science and Technology industry. Mandated to create a new order of dynamism and creativity, we delve deep within potentials of individuals, creating a pro active optimistic culture towards every Endeavour and life matters in general. Founded by Gabriel Jah in the year 2012, he ran the Company as a sole proprietor from 2012-2015 and in 2015 it became a cooperative involving other creative skilled individuals in other relative sectors. The term "GEE" represents, GODLY, EXCELLENT & EXTRAORDINARY and therefore we reckon with the fact that we are Godly people with excellent mindsets always working towards the extra-ordinary.

Due to its versatile nature, the company has a record label which entails the entertainment and multimedia dimension of the Company and is termed Geenation Records. At Geenation records we solicit for talented, skilled and creative individuals with raw or refined talents, inculcating and bequeathing a genius frame of mind in them, creating diverse platforms for them to fly and make fortune in their respective musical accomplishments .

At Geenation Records, every artist is given the freedom of expression to demonstrate their distinguishing abilities, we showcase them before their world, creating a fan base for them, strategizing and equipping them with what it takes to ascertain a sustainable musical career that will stand the cause of time.

In ICT and technology, we strive to invent new methodology for creating outstanding inventions, softwares, apps and other related ICT/Multimedia projects to heal the world and make it a better place for mankind.

Geenation has her initiation from 1st peter 2:9 which affirms our state as a chosen generation, a royal priesthood a holy Nation, a Peculiar people to show forth the praises of him who has called us, out of darkness into his marvelous light.
