GEEGUIDE

Geeguide is a tutoring and Learning platform for ICT and MULTIMEDIA interested individuals to learn Courses of their choices, share knowledge and get certified at a very minimal cost.

For every session we bring various experienced individuals from divers fields of ICT and MULTIMEDIA to lecture students in their respective areas of expertise. These courses range from:

Graphic Design, 3D Animation, Programming, Broadcasting, Sound Engineering, Cinematography, Mixing and Mastering, Music Production, Website Design, Music, Digital Marketing, App development and so on. Our Aim is to inculcate a default creative frame of mind amongst individuals, giving them an edge in ICT and relevance in Multimedia

What We Do

We teach in practical and theories at designated locations, giving you that classroom experience and personal physical rapport with lecturers and resource persons. We also create room for busy scheduled individuals, we carry out home tutoring services, weekend deals and online lectures. We have learning aids and materials such as Tutorial Videos, PDFs and handbooks to complement learning. We also give our student the chance to visit multimedia Houses Such as TV stations, Recording Studios, Movie Set Locations, e.t.c and practice what they are learning or learnt.
